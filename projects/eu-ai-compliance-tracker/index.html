📅 EU AI Act Compliance Timeline
Feb 2, 2025 ✓ PASSED
Prohibited AI Systems Ban PROHIBITED
Social scoring, real-time biometric surveillance, and manipulative AI systems are now illegal in the EU.
May 2, 2025 ✓ PASSED
GPAI Code of Practice GPAI
General-purpose AI models (like GPT, Claude, Gemini) must comply with transparency and copyright rules. Systemic risk models need safety evaluations.
Aug 2, 2026 ⚡ CRITICAL — 70 DAYS
High-Risk AI Full Compliance HIGH RISK
All high-risk AI systems (recruitment, credit scoring, medical devices, critical infrastructure, biometric ID) must complete conformity assessment, EU database registration, risk management system, and human oversight measures.
Aug 2, 2027
Product Safety Annex I Systems EXTENDED
AI systems covered under Annex I product safety legislation get an additional year to comply.
🏷️ AI Risk Categories & Fines
🚫 Prohibited
Unacceptable Risk

Social credit scoring · Subliminal manipulation · Real-time biometric surveillance · Emotion recognition at work/school
⚠️ High Risk
Regulated Use
Fine: up to €15M / 3% revenue
Recruitment AI · Credit scoring · Medical diagnosis · Critical infrastructure · Law enforcement · Border control
💬 Limited Risk
Transparency Required
Fine: up to €7.5M / 1.5% revenue
Chatbots (must disclose AI) · Deep fakes · Emotion recognition systems
✅ Minimal Risk
Voluntary Code of Practice
No mandatory obligations
Spam filters · AI video games · Inventory management · Manufacturing quality control
